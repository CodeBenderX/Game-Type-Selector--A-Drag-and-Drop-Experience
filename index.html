Welcome to Mobile Legends

Classic Game

Classic Mode in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang is a traditional 5v5 battle where two teams strive to destroy the enemy's base while defending their own. Players select from a diverse roster of heroes, engaging in strategic, fast-paced matches that last around 10-15 minutes.

Ranked Game

Ranked Game in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang is a competitive 5v5 mode where players battle to climb the ranks, from Warrior to Mythic, by winning matches and earning stars. Each season, players aim to showcase their skills, strategic teamwork, and hero mastery to achieve higher rankings and exclusive rewards.

Arcade Mode

Arcade Mode in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang offers various fun and unique game modes, such as Mayhem, Mirror, and Brawl, providing a refreshing break from standard gameplay. These modes feature special rules and mechanics, allowing players to enjoy a diverse and entertaining experience with shorter match durations and different strategic challenges.

Magic Chess

Magic Chess in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang is an auto-battler mode where players strategically position heroes on a chessboard, combining their unique abilities to form powerful synergies. Matches involve eight players competing in rounds, with the goal of being the last one standing by tactically upgrading heroes and optimizing team compositions.

Brawl Mode

Brawl Mode in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang is a fast-paced 5v5 game mode played on a single lane map, emphasizing intense and continuous combat. Players choose from a limited hero pool and engage in quick, action-packed matches, making strategic decisions with fewer resources and a heightened focus on teamwork and hero skills.

Versus AI

Human vs. AI in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang allows players to team up and battle against computer-controlled opponents, providing a practice ground to refine strategies and hero skills. This mode is ideal for learning game mechanics, testing new heroes, and preparing for competitive play in a less pressured environment.

Practice Mode

Practice Mode in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang is designed for players to hone their skills, experiment with different heroes, and test various strategies in a controlled environment. It provides unlimited resources and customizable settings, allowing players to freely explore game mechanics and improve their gameplay without the pressure of a live match.
